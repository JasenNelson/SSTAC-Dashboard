## 2025-03-31 - Missing aria-label on icon-only buttons with titles
**Learning:** Found a pattern across multiple components (QRCodeModal, PollNavigationButtons, FilterSidebar, ResultsDisplay) where icon-only buttons used `title` attributes for tooltips but were missing `aria-label`s for screen readers and `focus-visible` styles for keyboard navigation. While `title` can provide an accessible name in some contexts, it's inconsistent across screen readers compared to an explicit `aria-label`. Moreover, the lack of focus indicators harms keyboard users trying to navigate the complex dashboard.
**Action:** When adding new icon-only buttons or interactive elements, always ensure they have an explicit `aria-label` (even if a `title` is present) and distinct `focus-visible` styles (using Tailwind classes like `focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2`) to ensure they are fully accessible to both screen reader and keyboard-only users.
